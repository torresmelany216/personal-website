Meet Melany

The girl behind
the camera.

I'm Melany Torres — 21, Mexican-American, Chicago born and raised in Bogan/Cragin. My family is from Mexico, and that culture is woven into everything I am and everything I make. Family isn't just something I value — it's my foundation.

"I create for the girls who grew up between two worlds and are still figuring out how to carry both."

I'm a junior studying Advertising at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign — a first-gen student who packed up her whole life and moved hours from home to build something. I document all of it: the homesickness, the growth, the matcha runs, the travels to San Francisco, Phoenix, and Mexico. The real stuff.

My Story

I've been a creator
since before I knew
what that meant.

At 14 years old, I secretly started a YouTube channel — no guide, no budget, just a camera and something to say. By the time I was 15, I had 10,000 subscribers. Real people, real growth, real community. Built completely on my own.

Life pulled me away from it. But content never left me. Last year I came back — with a plan, a purpose, and a community that was waiting. Now I'm at 15K+ across platforms and building toward the full-time creator career I've always known was mine.
